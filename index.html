Abstract.

This abstract summarizes our entry in an online data science competition on DrivenData.org called “Pump it Up: Data Mining the Water Table”. The goal of the competition is to predict the status of water wells in Tanzania. Using the original datasets from the competition, we applied a set of classifiers including k-nearest neighbor, support vector machines, Naive Bayes, Decision trees, and Logistic regression with varying degrees of success. Ultimately, an ensemble classifier produced our best prediction score of 0.8213, placing us 34th out of 1550 teams on the Pump it Up Data science competition leaderboard as of May 25, 2016.
